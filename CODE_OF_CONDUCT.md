# Transport Stack Code of Conduct

We believe trust, respect, collaboration, and transparency are core values for a healthy open-source community. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for contributors and maintainers.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to:
- Foster an open, inclusive, and harassment-free environment
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Recognize the effort and contributions of all participants

## Expected Behavior

All participants should:
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Use welcoming language
- Give and accept constructive feedback
- Apologize and take responsibility when mistakes are made

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Offensive or sexual language, gestures, or imagery
- Personal attacks or harassment
- Publishing others’ private information without consent
- Dismissing or mocking inclusion-oriented efforts
- Threats, intimidation, or inciting violence

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards. They may take any action deemed appropriate, including warnings or removal from the community.

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to:

📧 [info@transportstack.in](mailto:info@transportstack.in)

Alternatively, you can open an issue using our [Bug Report template](./issue-templates/bug.md) and mark it as a Code of Conduct violation.

All reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly.

For security-related concerns, please refer to our [Security Policy](./SECURITY.md).
